How to Pass Any Google Cloud Certification (Architect, DevOps, Developer)

A proven 5-step method to pass any Google Cloud certification, based on real experience.

The method

After passing three of the most important Google Cloud certifications, I'm sharing the exact method I used. It's not magic: it's a structured process that works if you follow it with discipline.

Step 1: Read the official exam guide

It sounds obvious, but most people skip it. The official exam documentation is your map. Without it, you can't structure your learning.

It contains:

  • The full syllabus
  • The weight of each section
  • What they actually evaluate
If you don't start there, you're studying blind.

Step 2: Udemy for didactic theory

Once you know what you need to learn, you need a solid theoretical foundation. Udemy is great for this:

  • Step-by-step video explanations
  • Clear examples
  • Logical topic ordering

It gives you context before practicing. Don't jump into labs without understanding the concepts first.

Step 3: Google Cloud Skills Boost

One of the most essential resources. The hands-on labs let you practice in real GCP environments without risk and without initial cost.

When you sign up for the first time, you get access to all labs for free.

Step 4: Use AI

Artificial intelligence is your best ally during study:

Real-time doubts

For any doubt while studying, ask it right away. Don't lose your flow.

Flash cards

At the end of each study session, ask your AI to create flash cards based on what you just studied.

This will make a real difference when memorizing new concepts. If you're a student, you get Gemini for free.

Step 5: ExamTopics

If you want to practice with real exam questions, ExamTopics is the key tool.

Yes, it's paid. Yes, it's worth paying for 1 month. You can also split the cost with other people.

Pros and cons

Pros

  • Clear structure: you know exactly what to do at each phase.
  • Accessible resources: most are free or low-cost.
  • Proven: works for any GCP certification.

Cons

  • Requires discipline: no shortcuts, you must follow the process.
  • ExamTopics is paid: worth it, but an additional cost.
  • Time: depending on your level, it may take weeks of preparation.

Conclusion

Apply this method and I guarantee you'll pass. It's not about studying more hours, but about studying with structure. The combination of theory, lab practice and real questions is what makes the difference.

Save this article if you're preparing for any Google Cloud certification: Professional Cloud Architect, Professional Cloud DevOps Engineer, Professional Cloud Developer, or any other.
